A scalable ASL recognition platform built with Python, PyTorch, Azure ML, Azure Blob Storage, OpenCV, and LangChain. It unifies WLASL, ASL Citizen, and ASL Alphabet data into a cloud-backed training workflow with 100K+ labeled samples and 2,000+ sign classes.

  • Designed Azure ML GPU training with distributed data loading from Azure Blob Storage, reducing per-epoch training time by over 60% versus local runs.
  • Built a frozen ResNet50, temporal transformer, BiGRU, and attention pooling architecture under a 4GB VRAM constraint.
  • Addressed a 10:1 class imbalance with mixup augmentation and weighted sampling, improving minority-class recall by about 15%.
  • Resolved production inference issues including sklearn version mismatches and sigmoid overflow in the hand tracker across three environments.
