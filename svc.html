Community Service

Giving back.

Volunteering experiences that shaped how I think about people, impact, and the value of showing up in the classroom, in the field, and in the community.

Education | Austin, TX | 2022

Leadership Intern - Bear Creek Elementary

As a volunteer at Bear Creek Elementary through AIAA, I introduced children to the fascinating world of STEM. Working alongside teachers, I helped students tackle hands-on projects from building simple circuits to science experiments and focused on making complex ideas approachable and exciting. I assisted in classroom management and lesson preparation for 5+ teachers, boosting student engagement by 15% and participation by 30%.

Agriculture | Community

Farm Volunteer

Volunteering at a local farm was a genuinely humbling experience. Working alongside farmers from early morning, planting seeds, weeding rows, and harvesting fresh vegetables gave me a deep respect for the land and the effort behind every meal. The produce went directly back to the local community, and that closed loop from effort to impact made every tired evening worth it.
